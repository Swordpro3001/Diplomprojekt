Meet the Team

AI4Ads is a student project at the Technologisches Gewerbemuseum (TGM) in Vienna. We use AI and data science to improve urban mobility and media planning. Our team combines expertise in data science, DevOps, and project management for innovative solutions.

Who We Are

We are students from class 5BHIT, working together on data analysis, AI model development, and scalable deployment to solve real-world challenges.
